what this is


"Nothing is a mistake. There’s no win and no fail, there’s only make."

—#6 in Corita Kent's rules for teachers and students, for a class she taught in 1967-1968.


We are an international group of people working in a range of mediums. We meet remotely once a week as a way to structure time for creative projects and play.

Our current schedule:

SUNDAYS
12PM ET / 18 CET : Write in to the group chat to define your intentions, then begin work.
2:30PM ET / 20:30 CET : End of the make session, sometimes followed by a virtual gathering.

Sessions are also sometimes used for giving/receiving feedback on projects.
